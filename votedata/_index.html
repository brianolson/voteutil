RCV Election Data

Data from as many sources as I could find of elections officials running RCV elections who publish "cast vote record" election data.

Votes have been converted from a variety of formats to files with one vote per line with the vote as application/x-www-form-urlencoded, e.g.:

Alice+Springs=1&Bob+Howard=2&Carol+Singer=3
